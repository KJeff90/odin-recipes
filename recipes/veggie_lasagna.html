Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Broccolini (All veggies are to be fresh)
  • Spinach
  • Cucumber
  • Cauliflower
  • Carrots
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Mozarella Cheese
  • Four Cheese Mexican Blend

Steps

Once all the veggies have been successfully washed, sliced, and prepped, we can now begin crafting this beautiful dish.

  1. Line the bottom of the pan with pasta sauce
  2. Add a layer of pasta noodles
  3. Proceed with a layer of ricotta cheese
  4. Layer in half of your fillings
  5. Sprinkle a generous amount of all three cheeses
  6. Repeat all these steps an additional time and you're ready for the oven!
